# Isotope Web Design — Product Context

## Users
Primary: owners of small home improvement businesses (plumbers, HVAC contractors, electricians, landscapers). They are busy, practical, and skeptical. They've seen overpriced agency quotes and low-quality freelancer work. They need someone who can explain what they're getting, why it's worth it, and that it won't break the bank.

Secondary: any small business owner who finds the site via referral or search.

## Product Purpose
A marketing website that generates inbound leads (quote requests). Conversion is the only success metric.

## Brand Voice
Direct, confident, plain-spoken. No jargon. No "custom digital solutions." Say what you do and what it costs. Build trust through transparency and specificity.

## Register
brand

## Anti-References
- Agency sites that hide pricing behind "let's talk"
- Cluttered portfolios with 20 case studies
- Generic SaaS-cream aesthetics (beige + Inter + blue CTA)
- Gradient text
- Glassmorphism cards
- Side-stripe accent borders

## Strategic Principles
- Every page has one CTA: Get a Quote
- Trust before features. Explain who you are before what you sell.
- Cost-effective doesn't mean cheap — position it as "smart business structure" not "budget option"
