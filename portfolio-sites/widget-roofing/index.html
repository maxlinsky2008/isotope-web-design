Our story · Since 1965

Three generations
on Atlanta rooftops.

Walter Widget hung his first ladder over a Grant Park bungalow in 1965. Six decades later, his grandkids still climb every roof we sign. The trucks got newer and the shingles got smarter, but the handshake has not changed.

We are not the biggest roofer in Georgia, and we like it that way. Staying family run means the person who quotes your roof is the person who stands behind it, through every storm season, for as long as you own the house.
