What we do

Real Cuban food, handled start to finish

Catering started the way everything here does: a regular asked Mireya to cook for her daughter’s quinceañera, and word got around. Fifteen years later we cater hundreds of events a year, and we still cook every one like it’s family.

We handle drop-off platters for the office, fully-staffed buffets for weddings, and our showstopper, a whole lechón roasted on-site while your guests watch. Tell us the date and the headcount; we’ll handle the rest.

Good to know

Catering questions, answered

Two weeks is ideal, especially for weekends and the on-site lechón roast. That said, we keep room for last-minute orders, so call us even if your event is days away and we’ll do our best.

Drop-off catering starts at 25 guests. Full-service and on-site roasts start at 40. For smaller gatherings, our regular family-style platters from the menu are a great option.

Absolutely. Much of our menu is naturally gluten-free, and we offer vegetarian and vegan mains like berenjena guisada and our chickpea stew. Tell us about allergies up front and we’ll build the menu around them.

Yes. Our Fiesta and Lechón packages include servers, chafing equipment, and setup & cleanup. We can also arrange tables, linens, glassware, and a live son trio through our partners.

All of Tampa Bay, including St. Petersburg and Clearwater. Delivery within Hillsborough County is included; a modest travel fee applies beyond it. Ask us about your venue.

Requesting a quote is free with no commitment. To reserve your date we ask for a 25% deposit, with the balance due the week of the event. We accept cards, ACH, and checks.
