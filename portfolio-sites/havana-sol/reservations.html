Private dining

La Sala: our room, your night

Behind the teal curtain is La Sala, our private dining room. It seats up to 40 for a plated dinner or 60 standing for a reception, with its own bar and space for a trio in the corner.

Rehearsal dinners, milestone birthdays, company nights, baby showers. Lucía will design a family-style menu around your group, and our team handles everything from the flowers to the last cafecito.

  • Seats 40 plated · 60 reception
  • Private bar & custom menu
  • Live music available on request
