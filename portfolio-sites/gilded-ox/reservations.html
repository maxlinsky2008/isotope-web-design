Good to know

Smart, elegant attire. Jackets are welcome but not required. We ask that parties of seven or more inquire through private dining.

Cancellations

Plans change. We simply ask for twenty-four hours' notice so we can offer the table to another guest.

Large parties

Celebrating with a crowd? Our private rooms seat up to ninety. Plan a private dinner.
