Our Story

A room with a century in its walls

The Gilded Ox occupies a restored 1887 building on Peachtree Road, once a private picture gallery for one of Atlanta's railroad families. We kept the plaster cornices, the oak floors, and the tall north light, then hung the walls with the art that gives us our name.

Proprietor Eleanor Vance assembled the collection across two decades. Executive Chef Soren Whitfield runs the hearth and the dry-age cellar below. Together they built the table they always wanted to be seated at.
