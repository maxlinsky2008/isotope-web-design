Our story

It started with one tired back.

Knead ATL began when our founder, a former physical therapist, got fed up with massage that felt either clinical or kitschy. No dim caves, no whale sounds, no mystery fees. Just skilled hands in a bright, welcoming room.

Ten years later we are still in Midtown, still family run, and still obsessed with the small things: warm linens, real conversation, and pressure dialed exactly where you need it.
