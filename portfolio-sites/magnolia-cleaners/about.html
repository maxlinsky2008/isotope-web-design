Our story

Three generations of careful hands

What started as a single storefront on Magnolia Row has grown into Atlanta's most-trusted neighborhood cleaner, without ever losing the personal touch.

Since 1998

A family business, built on trust

When Rosa and James Whitfield opened Magnolia Cleaners in 1998, they had one rule: treat every garment like it belongs to someone you love. Twenty-five years later, their daughter Maya runs the front counter, and that rule hasn't changed.

We've pressed prom dresses that became wedding gowns, rescued heirloom quilts, and gotten more than a few grass stains out of more than a few Little League uniforms. Atlanta has trusted us with the pieces that matter, and we don't take that lightly.
