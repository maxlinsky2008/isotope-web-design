Who We Are

Our Mission

ABC Plumbing was founded in 1998 by Robert and Linda Chen, two licensed plumbers who believed that great service and honest pricing shouldn't be rare in this industry. Starting with a single truck and a residential focus, we grew steadily by doing one thing exceptionally well: showing up on time, diagnosing accurately, and fixing it right the first time.

Our mission has never changed. We exist to give homeowners and business owners complete peace of mind about their plumbing systems. That means no upselling work that isn't needed, no vague estimates that balloon into surprises, and no technicians who don't treat your home with respect.

Today our team of 30+ certified plumbers serves over 12 communities in the greater Metro area, handling everything from a dripping faucet to full commercial system builds — with the same care and precision that started this company.

Our History

From a two-person operation to the region's most trusted plumbing company, built on 25 years of consistent service.

1998

Founded in Oak Park

Robert and Linda Chen launch ABC Plumbing with one service van, focusing entirely on residential repair in the Oak Park community.

2004

Expanded to 5 Technicians

Word-of-mouth growth allows ABC to hire its first four employees and expand service to neighboring River Forest and Elmwood Park.

2010

Launched Commercial Division

Growing demand from local businesses prompted ABC to build a dedicated commercial plumbing team, now serving restaurants, offices, and multi-unit buildings.

2018

BBB Accredited A+ Rating

ABC Plumbing earned its first BBB A+ accreditation and was recognized as a Local Business Award finalist, cementing its reputation across the Metro area.

Today

30+ Technicians, 12 Cities

With a growing team and a 4.9-star average across over 1,200 reviews, ABC Plumbing continues to set the standard for reliable plumbing service in the greater Metro area.

More Than a Business

Building Our Community

ABC Plumbing has always believed that a strong business is inseparable from a strong community. We're active supporters of local youth sports leagues, annual home repair programs for seniors, and the Oak Park Business Association.

Every year we participate in "Fix It Forward" — a program where our technicians volunteer their time to repair plumbing issues in low-income households at no cost. Last year alone we completed over 80 free service calls through this initiative.

We also offer apprenticeship placements for local trade school graduates, giving the next generation of plumbers a chance to learn under IAPMO-certified professionals in a real-world environment.
