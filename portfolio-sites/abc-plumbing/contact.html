Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the questions we hear most often.

Do you offer free estimates?

Yes — we provide a written estimate at no cost before any work begins. For diagnostic visits where troubleshooting is required, a $59 diagnostic fee applies, which is waived when you proceed with the repair.

How quickly can you respond to an emergency?

Our average emergency response time is 47 minutes. We guarantee dispatch within 60 minutes of your call, 24 hours a day, 7 days a week — including all holidays.

Are your technicians licensed and background checked?

Every ABC Plumbing technician holds a current state plumbing license, passes an IAPMO certification process, undergoes a comprehensive background check, and is drug-screened before their first day on the job.

What warranty do you offer on your work?

We back all labor with a 2-year warranty. Manufacturer warranties on parts (water heaters, fixtures, etc.) are transferred directly to you and typically range from 1–10 years depending on the product.

What forms of payment do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, check, and cash. Financing options are available for larger projects through our approved lending partners — ask your technician for details.
