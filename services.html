What we offer

Services

Here's everything that goes into your site. I build it all up front for free — you only pay a one-time launch fee plus a simple monthly plan once you're ready to go live.

01

Website Design

A custom layout built around your brand, your audience, and what you want them to do.

What you get

Your website is often the first thing a potential customer sees. It needs to look credible and professional in seconds. I design layouts that are modern, organized, and built specifically for your business — not recycled from a generic template.

Every design is built mobile-first, so it looks great whether your customer is checking you out on a job site from their phone or sitting at a desk.

  • Custom page layouts for every section of your site
  • Mobile-first responsive design
  • Brand color and typography application
  • Visual hierarchy that guides visitors toward your contact form
  • Up to 2 design revisions before development begins

02

Website Development

Clean, hand-coded HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Fast by default, no bloat.

No page builders. No bloat.

Sites built on drag-and-drop builders like Wix or Squarespace carry a lot of hidden weight — slow load times, poor SEO, and limitations you only discover later. I write code by hand, which means your site is lean, fast, and built exactly the way it should be.

Fast sites rank better on Google, convert better on mobile, and don't require a monthly subscription to a platform you don't own.

  • Hand-coded HTML, CSS, and JavaScript
  • Optimized images and assets for fast load times
  • Cross-browser tested (Chrome, Safari, Firefox, Edge)
  • Contact form with validation
  • Deployment to a hosting provider of your choice

03

Branding & Logos

A logo and visual identity that make your business look like it belongs at the top.

Look the part

A strong brand isn't just about having a nice logo — it's about consistency. When your truck wrap, your website, and your business card all use the same colors, fonts, and mark, customers remember you. I build complete identity systems, not just a single file.

Everything is delivered in formats you can actually use: vector files for print, PNGs for the web, and a simple brand guide so you always apply it correctly.

  • Primary logomark and wordmark
  • Brand color palette with hex and CMYK values
  • Typography selection and usage guide
  • Deliverables in SVG, PNG, and PDF formats
  • Simple one-page brand guide

04

SEO Basics

The foundational setup that lets Google find your business and show it to local customers.

Get found locally

Most small local businesses don't need a complex SEO campaign — they need the basics done correctly. That means your pages have the right titles, descriptions, and structure so Google understands what you do and where you do it.

I also set up or optimize your Google Business Profile so your business shows up in local map searches, which is where most service-based searches end up.

  • On-page SEO: titles, meta descriptions, heading structure
  • Google Business Profile setup or optimization
  • Basic local keyword targeting
  • XML sitemap and robots.txt configuration
  • Page speed optimization for Core Web Vitals

05

Maintenance & Hosting Help

Keep your site up, secure, and current without having to think about it.

Set it and forget it

Once your site is live, you shouldn't have to worry about it going down, getting hacked, or becoming outdated. I handle ongoing updates, security monitoring, and minor content changes so you can stay focused on your work.

This is the monthly plan that keeps your site live after launch — reliable hosting, ongoing support, and updates at one simple flat rate. No proprietary platform lock-in, and you can walk away whenever you like.

  • Monthly content updates (text, images, hours, etc.)
  • Security monitoring and patch management
  • Uptime monitoring with alerts
  • Hosting setup and configuration guidance
  • Priority support for any issues that come up
